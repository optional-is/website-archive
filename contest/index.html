Mailing List

Even though the contest is quiet, we'll still be sending out our Quarterly newsletters. Feel free to subscribe.

How to win

You need to send your latitude and longitude by moving and zooming the map so the cross-hairs are centered on your location. The lat/lon is used to randomly pick the winner and the email address is so we can contact you. That's it. You don't need to spam 6 friends or retweet or follow us.

The only thing we ask in return is that you subscribe the mailing list. We send out one single message each quarter and if you don't like it you can unsubscribe after winning, you won't hurt our feelings, well not too much at least.

The winner will be drawn 2 months through the quarter. The submitted point closest to the randomly chosen point, according to the Haversine Formula will be deemed the winner.

Note: If you win, we'll send you an email to get your full address. If your address isn't nearby the lat/lon you sent in, we'll pick a new random point. (So don't get smart and submit 50 times with 50 different locations, then ask us to send it elsewhere.)
