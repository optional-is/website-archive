Correlation is not Causation

This is an interesting example of correlation and not causation. As each successive wave of COVID passed through the US population, the reviews of Yankee Candles trended to 1-star. People would complain about the lack of scent, but they hadn't yet realized that one symptom of COVID-19 is loss of smell.

It's interesting to think about which unexpected and seemingly unrelated online activities could be a lead indicator of public health concerns.
