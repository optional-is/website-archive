Mastodon Move

When Twitter collapsed, we joined a small, local, Icelandic instance. This is exactly what Mastodon is designed for: a local feed of like-minded people. This was our way to get back into the Icelandic community.

We looked into running our own personal instance, but the maintenance, storage and bandwidth put us off. We suspect that a lot of early adopters made the jump before realizing the effort and commitment needed. Fast-forward a few years, the dust has settled and instances are closing.

We're sad to see our local community Mastodon instance close.

You can now find us over at @briansuda@mastodon.social

Chimborazo

Chimborazo is the highest mountain in Ecuador with an elevation of 6,263m (20,548ft). If measured, not from sea level, but from the center of the Earth, Chimborazo is 2.1 km (1.3 mi) farther than the summit of Everest. This is due to Earth's equatorial bulge.

Does that make Chimborazo Earth's tallest mountain?
