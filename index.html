Our Work

Over the years we've had the good fortune to work with lots of great companies. From creating dashboards to problem solving, we help with strategy and special projects. Our clients include the PwC USA, TestFlight/Apple, UEFA, Marel, City of Reykjavík and many smaller companies.

We bring past experience working at TM-Software/Nýherji, CLARA and Skólapúlsinn on projects ranging from Icelandair Boarding pass design, large scale natural language text processing and data visualizations to making an impact in the educational section through online surveys, analysis and reporting.

If you think we can help you, get in contact and we'll find a time to chat.

PwC

At CLARA we developed an online tool to help companies monitor the discussion about their brands on social media, news sites and forums through easy to use data visualizations and dashboards.

PwC

We worked with PwC for close to a year. Building-up their internal data vizualization team and creating beautiful, mobile friendly, internal dashboards.

UEFA Calendar

For UEFA, we created a fully-customizable online calendar tool to follow teams, leagues and events so you'll never miss a game.

TestFlight

Working with the team at Burstly, we prototyped several iterations of their popular TestFlight tool and unreleased FlightPath service before they were acquired by Apple.
